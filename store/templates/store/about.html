At Bhawana Handloom, we believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it’s a reflection of culture, comfort, and confidence. Founded by Manish Panjwani, our journey began with a passion for crafting high-quality ethnic wear that blends traditional artistry with contemporary trends. We specialize in manufacturing and wholesaling kurtis and co-ord sets, designed to suit the modern woman while preserving the elegance of Indian craftsmanship. Every piece is thoughtfully created with premium fabrics, vibrant colors, and intricate detailing to ensure both style and durability. As a manufacturer, we take pride in our in-house production process, which allows us to maintain strict quality control, offer competitive pricing, and deliver bulk orders on time. As a wholesaler, we cater to retailers, boutiques, and fashion businesses across India, helping them bring the best in women’s ethnic wear to their customers. Our mission is simple—to make timeless fashion accessible to all while supporting local artisans and promoting the handloom tradition. Whether you're looking for everyday wear, festive attire, or statement pieces, Bhawana Handloom promises clothing that speaks elegance, comfort, and quality. Join us in celebrating style, tradition, and the art of handloom.

Mission

To be a leading name in women's ethnic fashion, blending tradition with innovation. We aim to inspire confidence through clothing that reflects elegance, comfort, and cultural heritage, while expanding our reach globally and setting new standards in quality, creativity, and sustainable practices within the fashion industry.

Vision

To design and deliver premium-quality kurtis and co-ord sets that combine style, durability, and affordability. We are committed to supporting local artisans, preserving traditional weaving techniques, and ensuring customer satisfaction through innovative designs, ethical practices, and timely delivery to wholesalers, retailers, and fashion partners across India and beyond.

Aim

To consistently provide trend-forward ethnic wear that meets evolving customer needs. We strive to strengthen our wholesale network, maintain exceptional product quality, and promote Indian craftsmanship worldwide, while embracing sustainability and innovation, ensuring Bhawana Handloom remains a trusted, competitive, and customer-focused brand in the women’s ethnic fashion market.
